THE BIRTHDAY PLAYBOOK
1. The Button & Academy: Get the free "Birthday" button at City Hall. Then, hit the Animation Academy (DCA). It's a free drawing class where he'll leave with a hand-drawn souvenir.
2. Budget Treats: Grab Mickey Beignets. Bring your own candle. Pro-tip: Stop at Harbor Galley for warm cookies (shareable and cheap!).
3. Free Surprises: Ask for birthday stickers! Use the Play Disney App in Galaxy's Edge to "hack" panels—it's high-engagement fun for zero cost.

Director's Note: "A 10th birthday is a digital heirloom moment. Focus on the stickers, the buttons, and the atmosphere. The magic is in the details, not the price tag."

MASTERING THE LIGHTNING LANE
The Mammoth Meta:
- The Fiddle Fattle: Don't settle for a 6PM slot. Go to "Modify Plan" and refresh (pull down) until a sooner time pops up.
- The 119-Min Rule: If your LL is far out, set a phone timer for 119 mins. You can book your next one the second it rings.
- Pin Your Bangers: In the "My Day" tab, select only your top 5 rides to keep them at the top of your tip board.
- PhotoPass Value: Stop for every photographer! Multi Pass includes all downloads. Make these the main souvenir.

No-Pay Thrills:
- Radiator Springs: Use the Single Rider Line. Turns 2 hours into 20 mins.
- Rise of the Resistance: Either "Rope Drop" or ride during fireworks at 9 PM.
- Filler Strategy: Use the Railroad or Tiki Room to bridge gaps between Lightning Lanes.

THE ADVANCED PLAYBOOK (Mammoth Club Insider Intel)
1. The "Golden Hour" Rope Drop (Standby Only)
- The First Hour Rule: Do not use a Lightning Lane in the first 60 minutes of the day. Lines are naturally short. Hit the "Big Three" of a land (Space Mountain, Star Tours, Buzz) via the standby line.
- Save the Multi Pass: Save your first Lightning Lane selection for a return time around 10:00 AM. This lets you "stack" reservations for when the park hits peak capacity.
- Skip Peter Pan (at open): Unless you're at the very front of the rope, the line hits 45 minutes within three minutes of opening.

2. The "Must-See" 70th Anniversary Exclusives
- World of Color – Happiness: Catch the Muppets Pre-Show. Join the virtual queue at 12:00 PM sharp.
- Paint the Night Parade: Head to the Small World promenade for less crowded views.
- Character Cavalcade: Features rare characters like Duffy and ShellieMay.

3. Advanced Dining & Value Hacks
- The "Snack-Size" Monte Cristo: Get it at Royal Street Veranda as a cheaper "snack" portion.
- Cold Brew Black Caf (Galaxy's Edge): Head to Docking Bay 7 for faster caffeine and better value.
- The "Fresh" Popcorn Secret: The Silver Popcorn Cart at the end of Buena Vista Street (DCA) is the only one with fresh caramel corn made on-site.

4. Hidden Gems for "Pacing"
- The Beast's Library: Inside Sorcerer's Workshop (Animation Academy). Air-conditioned and usually empty.
- The Flag Retreat Ceremony: ~4:30 PM, Town Square. A classic old-school Disney moment.
- Magic Mirror (Animation Building): Free interactive character matching.

5. Final "Pro-Tip" for Efficiency
- The 119-Minute Alarm: Set a timer for 1 hour and 59 minutes after booking a far-out LL. Book a second LL the moment it goes off to hold two at once.
