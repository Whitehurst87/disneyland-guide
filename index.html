The Birthday
Playbook

1

The Button

Head to City Hall or any shop first thing Tuesday. Ask for a free "Happy Birthday" button. The Cast Members will wish him a happy birthday all day!

2

Budget Treats

Skip the $150 dinner. Grab a Dole Whip or Mickey Beignets. Bring your own small birthday candle for the perfect photo moment.

3

Free Surprises

Ask for birthday stickers! In Galaxy's Edge, mention the birthday to the Resistance or First Order—they often have special interactions.

Director's Note

"A 10th birthday is a digital heirloom moment. Focus on the stickers, the buttons, and the atmosphere. The magic is in the details, not the price tag."

Mastering the
Lightning Lane

The Tech Rules

  • ★ The 120-Min Rule: Book as soon as you tap in, OR every 2 hours. Set a phone timer.
  • ★ Free Photos: LLMP includes Unlimited PhotoPass. Stop at every photographer (Castle, Falcon, Cars Land)!
  • ★ Virtual Queue: Tiana's Bayou requires joining at 7 AM or 1 PM (at Airbnb) in the app.

No-Pay Thrills

Avoid paying extra for Individual Lightning Lanes (ILL):

  • ✔ Radiator Springs: Use the Single Rider Line. Turns 2 hours into 20 mins.
  • ✔ Rise of the Resistance: Either "Rope Drop" (arrive 45m early) or ride during fireworks at 9 PM.
